Story Submissions

This is the command and control quick moderation area for story submissions. In this area you can edit, delete or approve story submissions. Select any combination of deletes or approves and click the submit button.

Field Description
Edit Click this to edit the story. Note that saving an edited story submission will approve it unless you save it with the draft flag set
Title Title of the story
Date Date the story was submitted
Topic Topic the story was submitted to
Delete / Approve Radio buttons to select for delete or approval of the story submission
